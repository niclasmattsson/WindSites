Explanation of the geodata of renewable energies
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Technology	--	Renewable Energy Technology
Year		--	Year of commissioning
Power_kW	--	Installed plant power (nominal capacity) in kilowatt
Source		--	Type of energy source
Area_sqm	--	Area size of open-field photovoltaic (operating area) in square meters
Hub_m		--	Hub height in meters
Dia_m		--	Diameter of the rotor in meters
X_coord		--	Easting value (ETRS 1989)
Y_coord		--	Northing value	(ETRS 1989)
PS		--	Note whether data has been edited or has an implausible performance
